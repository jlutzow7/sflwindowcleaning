Solar Panel Cleaning

Dirty Panels Cost You Money Every Day

Solar panels are a significant investment, and most homeowners and business owners install them expecting years of reliable energy production. But the performance of a solar system depends almost entirely on how much sunlight reaches the photovoltaic cells, and anything sitting on the surface of the glass blocks that light.

In South Florida, panels face a particularly demanding environment. Salt air from the Atlantic and Gulf leaves a fine mineral film on panel surfaces that builds up over time. Pollen season deposits thick layers of fine dust across entire arrays. Runoff from summer storms leaves hard water minerals and sediment. Bird droppings create concentrated spots that block sunlight and can etch into panel glass if not removed promptly. And the intense subtropical sun bakes all of it onto the surface, making it harder to remove the longer it sits.

Rain alone isn't enough. While rain can rinse away loose dust, it also carries minerals and sediment from the atmosphere that dry onto the glass and leave residue behind. Panels that rely on rain alone typically underperform panels that receive professional maintenance, which is why regular solar panel maintenance and solar efficiency restoration through cleaning matter as much as the initial installation.

How Much Efficiency Are You Losing?

Solar industry research consistently shows that dirty panels can lose 15-25 percent of their energy output, and in coastal or high-pollen environments losses can be even higher. For a residential system, that lost output translates directly into a higher electric bill. For a commercial system, it means reduced return on a significant capital investment.

Regular professional cleaning restores that lost output and keeps your system performing the way it was designed to.

Our Method

Pure Water Cleaning — Safe, Effective, and Residue-Free

We use a pure water cleaning system for all solar panel cleaning. Ordinary tap water runs through a multi-stage filtration process that removes minerals, dissolved solids, and impurities before it ever contacts your panels. The purified water then actively draws contaminants off the panel surface and evaporates completely clean — no soap film, no mineral deposits, no residue of any kind.

We use soft, non-abrasive brushes designed specifically for solar panel surfaces. No harsh chemicals. No pressure washing. No abrasive pads or tools that could scratch or damage the glass coating on your panels.

Pre-Clean Inspection

Before any solar panel cleaning begins, we conduct a thorough visual inspection of the entire system. We look for and document any pre-existing conditions, including cracked panels, damaged framing, loose connections, compromised seals, or any other issues present before we arrive.

This protects you and it protects us. You get an honest assessment of your system's condition before cleaning starts, and any issues we find are clearly documented, so there's no question about what existed before our work began.

  • Pure water system — no chemicals, soaps, or detergents
  • Non-abrasive soft brushes, safe for all panel surfaces
  • No pressure washing — gentle rinse only
  • Pre-clean inspection documents existing conditions
  • Cracked, damaged, or loose panels noted before work begins
  • Spot-free, residue-free finish on all panel glass
  • Safe for all major panel brands and mounting systems
Why pure water? Because it leaves absolutely no residue behind. As purified water evaporates, it takes everything it lifted off the panel with it — no spots, no film, no residue. Your panels dry crystal clear.
