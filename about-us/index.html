About Us

South Florida Window Cleaning

South Florida Window Cleaning has been providing professional exterior cleaning services to residential and commercial customers throughout Broward, Palm Beach, and Miami-Dade Counties for nearly two decades. What started as a commitment to quality workmanship and dependable service has grown into a trusted name in exterior cleaning across South Florida, including Fort Lauderdale, Davie, and Cooper City.

We are a family-owned, owner-operated business. When you call, you're talking to the owner. When we show up at your property, the owner is there doing the work. That means you get a level of personal attention, accountability, and care that larger companies simply can't match.

Every property we service gets our full attention, whether it's a single-family home, an estate property, a commercial storefront, or a large commercial building. Our goal on every job is the same: do the work right, leave the property better than we found it, and earn the kind of trust that keeps customers coming back and referring us to their neighbors, friends, and colleagues.

Owner-Operated. Personally Accountable.

Being owner-operated means something specific to us. It means we don't send crews whose quality varies from visit to visit. It means when you have a question or a concern, you're reaching the person who actually did the work. It means we stand behind every job we do, because our name and our reputation are on the line every time.

No long-term contracts. We earn your trust on every visit. If you're not satisfied with the results, we make it right, no questions asked.

Professional Equipment. Safe Methods.

We invest in professional-grade equipment and stay current with the best methods in the industry. Our pure water cleaning system delivers spot-free, residue-free results on windows and solar panels. Our soft washing equipment uses biodegradable, environmentally-safe solutions that clean exterior surfaces thoroughly without damaging paint, stucco, or landscaping. Our pressure washing equipment is calibrated for each surface type, delivering effective cleaning without causing damage.

We take the time to assess each property before we start and use the right method for each surface. That attention to detail is what separates a professional result from an average one.
