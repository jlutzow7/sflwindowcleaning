About Us

South Florida Window Cleaning

South Florida Window Cleaning has been providing professional exterior cleaning services to residential and commercial customers throughout Broward, Palm Beach, and Miami-Dade Counties for nearly two decades. What started as a commitment to quality workmanship and dependable service has grown into a trusted name in exterior cleaning across South Florida.

We are a family owned and owner-operated business. When you call us, you talk to the owner. When we show up at your property, the owner is there doing the work. That means you get a level of personal attention, accountability, and care that larger companies simply cannot match.

Every property we service gets our full attention — whether it is a single-family home, a luxury estate, a storefront, or a large commercial building. Our goal on every job is the same: do the work right, leave the property better than we found it, and earn the kind of trust that keeps customers coming back and referring us to their neighbors, friends, and colleagues.

Owner-Operated. Personally Accountable.

Being owner-operated means something specific to us. It means we do not send out crews where the quality varies from visit to visit. It means when you have a question or a concern, you reach the person who actually did the work. It means we stand behind every job we do — because our name and our reputation are on the line every single time.

No long-term contracts. We earn your business on every visit. If you are not satisfied with the results, we make it right — no questions asked.

Professional Equipment. Safe Methods.

We invest in professional-grade equipment and stay current with the best methods in the industry. Our pure water cleaning system delivers streak-free, residue-free results on windows and solar panels. Our soft washing equipment uses environmentally safe, biodegradable solutions that clean exterior surfaces thoroughly without damaging paint, stucco, or landscaping. Our pressure washing equipment is calibrated for each surface type to deliver an effective clean without causing damage.

We take the time to assess each property before we begin and use the right method for each surface. That attention to detail is what separates a professional result from an average one.
