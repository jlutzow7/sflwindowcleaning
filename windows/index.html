Our Method

The Pure Water Difference

Most window cleaners use soap, detergents, or squeegee solutions. While that may sound clean, soap leaves an invisible residue on the glass that attracts dust, pollen, salt, and airborne debris — causing windows to look dirty again fast. In South Florida's humid, salty climate, that buildup happens faster than almost anywhere else.

Our pure water cleaning system runs ordinary tap water through a multi-stage filtration process that removes all minerals and impurities before it ever touches your windows. The result is water so pure it actively draws contaminants off the glass, frames, and surrounding edges — then evaporates completely clean.

No soap film. No mineral spots. No streaky residue. Just clean, clear glass with a bright natural finish that stays cleaner longer.

Pure water is also safer. Water-fed poles allow many upper-floor windows to be cleaned from the ground — no ladders against your home or business.

  • No soap residue to attract dust and dirt
  • Stays cleaner longer than traditional soap-based cleaning
  • Streak-free, spot-free finish on exterior glass
  • Zero chemicals — safe around kids, pets, and landscaping
  • Reaches many upper-floor windows safely from the ground
  • Cleans glass, frames, and surrounding edges simultaneously
  • Ideal for South Florida — salt air, pollen, storms, and heat

Residential Window Cleaning

Your Home Deserves a Crystal-Clear View

In South Florida, windows take a beating. Salt air from the Atlantic and Gulf leaves a white mineral haze on exterior glass. Afternoon thunderstorms bring pollen, dust, and hard water minerals. The intense subtropical sun bakes it all onto the surface — making glass look cloudy and dull even after ordinary cleaning.

We provide residential window cleaning for single-family homes, condos, townhomes, and estate properties throughout Broward, Palm Beach, and Miami-Dade Counties. Every visit includes frames, sills, screens, and accessible tracks — not just the glass.

Exterior Window Cleaning

We use our pure water system for exterior glass — lifting and rinsing away salt, pollen, mineral buildup, and debris without leaving soap residue. Water-fed poles allow us to reach many second-story windows safely from the ground.

Interior Window Cleaning

Interior cleaning uses a modified pure water method along with traditional detailing where needed. We work carefully around floors, furniture, and window treatments. Sliding glass doors, French doors, picture windows, and interior glass are cleaned to leave your view bright, clear, and natural.

  • Sliding glass doors, French doors, and impact windows
  • Screens cleaned and reinstalled
  • Frames, sills, and accessible tracks wiped down
  • High exterior windows reached safely from the ground
  • Skylights and specialty glass
  • Single-family homes, condos, townhomes, and estates
