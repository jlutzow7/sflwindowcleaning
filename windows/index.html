Our Method

The Pure Water Difference

Most window cleaners use soap, detergents, or squeegee solutions. While that may sound clean, soap leaves an invisible residue on the glass that attracts dust, pollen, salt, and airborne debris — causing windows to look dirty again fast. In South Florida's humid, salty climate, that buildup happens faster than almost anywhere else.

Our pure water cleaning system runs ordinary tap water through a multi-stage filtration process that removes all minerals and impurities before it ever touches your windows. The result is water so pure it actively draws contaminants off the glass, frames, and surrounding edges — then evaporates completely clean.

No soap film. No mineral spots. No streaky residue. Just clean, clear glass with a bright natural finish that stays cleaner longer.

Pure water is also safer. Water-fed poles allow many upper-floor windows to be cleaned from the ground — no ladders against your home or business.

  • No soap residue to attract dust and dirt
  • Stays cleaner longer than traditional soap-based cleaning
  • Streak-free, spot-free finish on exterior glass
  • Zero chemicals — safe around kids, pets, and landscaping
  • Reaches many upper-floor windows safely from the ground
  • Cleans glass, frames, and surrounding edges simultaneously
  • Ideal for South Florida — salt air, pollen, storms, and heat

FAQ

Window Cleaning Questions, Answered

How often should I have my windows cleaned in South Florida?

Most South Florida homes and businesses benefit from window cleaning every 1 to 3 months. Salt air, pollen, and frequent storms cause buildup faster than in most other climates, so many customers choose monthly or bi-monthly plans to stay ahead of it.

What is the pure water cleaning system?

Pure water cleaning runs tap water through a multi-stage filtration system that removes minerals and impurities before it touches your windows. Because the water is completely pure, it lifts dirt away and evaporates without leaving any soap film, streaks, or mineral spots behind.

Do you clean interior windows too?

Yes. We offer both interior and exterior window cleaning for residential and commercial properties, including sliding glass doors, French doors, and interior-facing glass.

Are screens and tracks included in window cleaning?

Yes. Every residential window cleaning visit includes frames, sills, screens, and accessible tracks — not just the glass.

Do you offer commercial window cleaning?

Yes. We provide commercial window cleaning near you for storefronts, office buildings, restaurants, medical offices, hotels, and HOA or property-managed buildings throughout Fort Lauderdale and South Florida, with flexible scheduling before or after business hours.

Is your window cleaning guaranteed streak-free?

Yes. Because our pure water system leaves zero soap residue or mineral spots behind, your windows dry completely clean and streak-free every time.
